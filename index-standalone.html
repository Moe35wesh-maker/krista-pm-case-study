üîç About Krista Platform

Krista is an AI-led intelligent automation platform that orchestrates people, systems, and AI agents through conversational interfaces. It combines RPA, iPaaS, BPM, and AI orchestration capabilities in a no-code platform designed for business users.

Key Capabilities

  • Conversational Automation: Natural language interface for building and interacting with workflows
  • No-Code Builder: Business users can create complex automations without programming
  • Integration Platform: 100+ pre-built connectors to enterprise applications
  • AI & Machine Learning: Document understanding, anomaly detection, predictive models
  • Enterprise Security: ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type 2, HIPAA compliant

Strategic Position

Krista occupies a unique position at the intersection of automation and AI orchestration. While competitors like UiPath focus on RPA and Microsoft focuses on their ecosystem, Krista enables platform-agnostic orchestration of people, systems, AND AI agents through an accessible conversational interface.

üóìÔ∏è 24-Month Strategic Roadmap

Phase 1: Foundation & Velocity (0-6 Months)

Theme: Reduce friction and accelerate self-serve adoption

Top Priorities:

  • Guided Onboarding Flow - Interactive walkthrough to reduce time-to-first-automation from 4-8 hours to <2 hours
  • Automation Template Library - 50+ pre-built templates across common use cases
  • Enhanced Core Integrations - Deep integrations for Salesforce, ServiceNow, Workday, Microsoft 365
  • Integration Health Dashboard - Real-time monitoring and diagnostics

Expected Impact: 40% reduction in time-to-first-automation, 2x increase in self-serve adoption, $1.2M+ ARR impact

Phase 2: Differentiation & Scale (6-12 Months)

Theme: Establish category leadership in AI agent orchestration

Top Priorities:

  • AI Agent Orchestration Hub - Centralized platform to deploy, monitor, and govern multiple AI agents
  • Community Template Marketplace - Enable users to publish, share, and monetize automation templates
  • Workflow Analytics & Process Mining - Advanced analytics showing automation ROI and bottlenecks
  • Advanced Security & Compliance - FedRAMP, enhanced RBAC, audit capabilities

Expected Impact: Category differentiation, $3.6M+ ARR impact, 125% Net Revenue Retention

Phase 3: Platform Maturity & Ecosystem (12-24 Months)

Theme: Become the AI automation operating system through ecosystem

Top Priorities:

  • Krista Developer Platform - APIs, SDKs, and partner ecosystem for third-party extensions
  • Industry Solution Packs - Pre-configured workflows for Healthcare, Financial Services, Manufacturing, Retail
  • AI Model Marketplace - Pluggable AI models for specialized tasks (fraud detection, sentiment analysis, etc.)
  • Global Multi-Region Deployment - EU and APAC regions for data sovereignty

Expected Impact: 25% partner-sourced revenue, $14M+ incremental ARR, sustainable platform moat

Key Trade-offs Made

  • Templates before AI features: Solves immediate adoption friction, creates compounding value through network effects
  • Agent orchestration before agent enhancements: Multi-agent management is emerging battleground; platform play wins long-term
  • Developer platform in Year 2: Ecosystem creates sustainable moat but requires team maturity first
